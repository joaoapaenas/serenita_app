# Serenita Study App

**Serenita** is a desktop application that helps you organize, plan, and track your study cycles. It was designed for students and professionals preparing for exams who want to manage their subjects, sessions, and progress in one clean and focused interface.

---

## üå± Main Features

-   **Study Cycles:** Build and manage study cycles with weighted subjects.
-   **Subject Management:** Add or edit subjects, assigning weights like relevance, volume, and difficulty.
-   **Progress Tracking:** See your study streaks, daily goals, and average performance.
-   **Performance Analytics:** Visualize how much you‚Äôve studied and where to focus next.
-   **Offline First:** Works fully offline ‚Äî all data is stored locally on your machine.

## üß≠ Getting Started

1.  **Create Your Profile:** On first launch, the app will guide you through creating a user profile.
2.  **Start a Cycle:** Define your first study cycle and add subjects to it.
3.  **Begin Studying:** The app will organize your study queue. Start a session and track your time.
4.  **Review Progress:** Check the analytics screen to see your performance.

All your data is saved automatically in a local database file (`serenita.db`).
